
> [!tip]  What is metabolism
> Metabolism is the chemical processes that take place in the _body_
> Why?
> - To sustain life
> - Produce energy

[[Biology MOC]]


Metabolism can be split into to _two main processes_:

##### Anabolism
<mark class="hltr-orange">Breaking down</mark> of substances. Examples of which are:
- Cellular Respiration
- Deamination

And,

##### Catabolism
<mark class="hltr-yellow">Building up</mark> of substances. Examples of which are:
- Photosynthesis
- Glycogen storage






### Metabolic wastes

Metabolic wastes are the main by-products of metabolic processes, some of which can be toxic to the body. Hence, it is the [[Excretory Processes]]'s function to remove such wastes and transport them away safely
Protein and nucleic acid metabolism produces nitrogenous wastes, such as:
- Ammonia
- Urea
- Uric Acid
