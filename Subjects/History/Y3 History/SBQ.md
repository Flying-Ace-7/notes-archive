M- <_Author_> wants <_target audience_> to know <_message_>



PEE:
- Point (Dont lift/paraphrase)
	- Use BEN (Beneficial, effective, necessary), to be Blamed, unfair, etc..
	- Dont describe things happening in the source
- Explain
	- _This shows that...._
- Evidence
	- _I know this as Source A shows "..."_
	- Quote from source (Lift is allowed)



Attitude keywords:

- Positive
	- Optimistic
	- Supportive
	- approving
	- Complimentary
- Negative
	- Pessimistic
	- unsupportive
	- Disapproving
	- Critical
	- Mocking/sarcastic
Note: Optimistic and pessimistic are in relation to the future

## Surprised

(1 Source)

Eg; Are you suprised by Source B? Explain

//1: Stand : 
	I am/ am not suprised by sourced B as .... (M) PEE 
(Note: The exp is to mention _Common sense thinking_)

//2: X-ref to other source / CK. 
	This is supported/challenged by CK/Source C , which shows (M), PEE. 
(Note: If support, the M has to be the same as the other source. If Challenged: The M has to be the **Direct opposite** of the other source)
**CONCLUDE:** I am not suprised/ suprised.

Support -> Not suprised
Challenge -> Suprised
