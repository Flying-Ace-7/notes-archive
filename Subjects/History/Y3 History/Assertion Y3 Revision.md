> Qn: Study all sources. "Statement" (e.g Ho Chi Minh is capable in leading the Vietcong ([[Decolonisation of vietnam]]) . How far to do the sources agree/support this view? Use the sources _and your knowldge_ to explain your answer. 
> When _and your knowledge_ is included, it tis likely that use of Contexual knowledge is required. Obtain form knowledge and/or background info.


Lets now explore the _types_ of statements that appear in these questions.

### Type 1: positive // negative impact (Benefit/disaster)

Eg. _American intervention was beneficial for Vietnam's Independance_.
So, lets observe how to write our paragraphs for this question.

We need two sources for **YES** and **NO**, choose sources from the given to do this. Decide wether a source answers yes or no.

Do one para for each source.

Have 2 yes paragraphs and 2 no paragraphs
#### Yes
- 1. STATE STAND (whether American intervention _was_ beneficial for Vietnam's Independance)
- Message -> Action (in source) relating to qn. It needs to be specific, and **not lifted** from the source. Needs _some_ CK to be used to get the specific detail of the source.
	- _For this question_: Military intervention due to the Gulf of tonkin resolution
- Evidence
	- _FTQ_: This is seen in Source A as it shows "evd"
- Explanation -> Positive Outcome(Depends on the qn, CHECK) -> Specific
	- _FTQ_: South Vietnam gained military strenght and political control.


##### No
- 1. STATE STAND
- 2. Message -> Action (Specifc), _similar to the Yes para_
- 3. Evidence
- 4. Explanation (Negative Outcome, again CHECK QN)
	- _FTQ_: Lose soverinity


### Type 2: Main factor in Qn?

_eg question: "The reason for <mark class="hltr-blue">American failure in Viet War</mark> was <mark class="hltr-red">due to it being a televised war</mark>"_ 

<mark class="hltr-red">Factor</mark>: The presence of media and use of media to televise the war
<mark class="hltr-blue">Outcome</mark>: American failure in Viet War.

> [!warning] Note: Cannot argue against the Outcome (Assumption) (eg. cannot say american success in Viet War)

#### Yes

- Message: Action (Factor in question, _FTQ_: Media) 
	- Since you cannot lift the factor into the Message directly, you need to use CK and details in the Message. -> What did it result in source
	- FTQ: Factor in qn. Investigatice journalism show **My Lai Massacre** (source) which result  in protest against USA Govt
- Explanation (<mark class="hltr-blue">Outcome</mark> in qn): 
	- _FTQ_: Led to USA Govt forced to enter negotiations, and lead to Vietnamisng the war. (Let South Vietnam Engage the Viet COng on their own,which would lead to their failure)

#### No:
- Message (SOME OTHER ACTION DIFFERING FROM YES PARAGRAPH)
	- _FTQ_: Locals support for the viet cong
- EXP: Outcome in qn.


In this type, the difference in the two paragraphs are the _ACTIONS_, not the _<mark class="hltr-cyan">outcome</mark>_
