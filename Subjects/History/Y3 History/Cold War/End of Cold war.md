### Afghan war

In 1970s, a soviet backed communist group was in power in afghanistan. They introduced reforms that went against the muslin culture of Afghanistan. The government became unpopulat. Opposition from afghan resistance, the _Mujahideen_. Civil war broke out.
CIA offered support to the Mujahideen for the next 10 years, Osama bin laden was part of the Mujahideen. (gasp)

-> The war crippled soviet economy, 1/4 of the Soviet unions GNP (gross national product) was used in the war

in 1989, USSR pulled out of Afghan.
 USA demanded that te Mujahideen give up their weapons, since that the war is over. They refused, and tensions rose. This conflict was seen as a holy war, and hence, USA vs Mujahideen began. the Mujahideen split into many wings, one of which was _Al-Qaeda_(9/11 boom)

### The Chernobyl Disaster, April 1986

In ukraine, they had a nuclear power plant active, was supposed to be checked regularly. However, one of them was malufunctioning, and the guy checking them _did not dare say that it was malufunctioning_. Higher ups also did not check it to confirm it, as they believed that he woud not dare to lie. In april 1986, the powerplant exploded, realeasing radioactive material into soviet union and parts of europe.  The explosion, fire and the release of radiation affected lives of hundreds of thousands of people(though less than a  100 people were killed.), and the area around the nuclear power plant will be contaminated for 24 000 years.

USSR covered up the event, and when other countries asked them the reason as to the radioactive material in their own country, USSR said it was a small explosion and it has been contained.
