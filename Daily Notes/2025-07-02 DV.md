### Procedure to test strength Of Glue

1. Make a hole in one of two blocks
2. Apply a drop of glue onto a block and glue it on to another block
3. Thread a string to the hole in one block, 
4. Hang weights of the string
5. Record the amount of mass it takes to break the bond between the blocks
